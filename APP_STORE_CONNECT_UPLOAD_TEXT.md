## App Description (full)

Real O Who is a private property marketplace for Australian buyers and sellers.  
List properties, browse and shortlist homes, submit and negotiate offers, and coordinate inspections and legal handoff workflows without agent-heavy process noise.

The app includes secure buyer-seller messaging and built-in communication safety controls (reporting and blocking), a legal workspace with invite links for conveyancers and solicitors, and in-app contract packet handoff steps so sale progress stays visible.

Data can operate with remote sync when the local backend is available, and all critical flows continue in local mode if needed.

3. Browse featured/private listings and open a listing detail.
4. Go to `Messages` and confirm secure conversation controls.
5. Go to a listing and submit an offer, then open the workspace.
6. Select a legal representative for buyer/seller flow and send invite code-based legal invite.
7. In `Account`, confirm support, privacy, terms, and delete account actions.
