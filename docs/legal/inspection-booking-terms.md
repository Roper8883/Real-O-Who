# Inspection Booking Terms

## Scope

Homeowner facilitates owner-managed open homes, private inspection requests, and related reminders.

## Booking process

Bookings may be requested, approved, rejected, cancelled, or rescheduled. Sellers remain responsible for access and safety arrangements unless an agent or other lawful representative is separately involved.

## User conduct

Attendees must behave lawfully and respectfully, follow reasonable property access instructions, and avoid recording or sharing personal information inappropriately.

## Cancellations and delays

Sellers and buyers should use the platform to manage timing updates promptly. Homeowner is not responsible for travel, opportunity, or other loss caused by changed plans except as required by law.
