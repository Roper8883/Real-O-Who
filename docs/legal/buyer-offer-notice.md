# Buyer Offer Notice

## Important notice

Offers made through Homeowner are not binding contracts by themselves.

## What an in-app offer means

An in-app offer is a recorded negotiation step. It may help the seller and buyer coordinate price, conditions, timing, and next actions, but it does not replace the legally correct contract process for the relevant jurisdiction.

## Independent advice

Buyers should obtain independent legal and conveyancing advice before relying on any offer, disclosure package, or document.

## Due diligence remains essential

Buyers remain responsible for finance, title, strata/body corporate, planning, zoning, contamination, building, pest, insurance, settlement, and any other property-specific due diligence unless the law states otherwise.

## Cooling-off varies

Cooling-off rights depend on the jurisdiction, transaction structure, and sale method. Buyers should not assume cooling-off applies in every situation.
