# Community Guidelines

## Respect first

Use the platform respectfully. Buyers, sellers, inspectors, and support staff should be able to interact without harassment or pressure.

## Prohibited conduct

Do not:

- threaten, intimidate, or harass
- use hateful, discriminatory, or abusive language
- mislead others about identity, authority, pricing, or property facts
- push scam tactics or off-platform pressure
- share sensitive personal information unnecessarily
- upload content you do not have rights to use

## Reporting and moderation

Users can report listings, messages, and conduct. Homeowner may review, remove, restrict, or suspend content and accounts where necessary to protect the platform and users.
