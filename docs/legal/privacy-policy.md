# Privacy Policy

## Status

Draft for legal review.

## What we collect

Homeowner may collect:

- account data such as name, email, phone number, password hash, and role
- profile data, including contact preferences and preferred locations
- identity verification data where verification is used
- property and listing data supplied by sellers
- communications data such as messages, inspection notes, and support requests
- inspection and booking data
- offer and transaction-progress data
- payment and invoice data for platform or service fees
- device, session, log, cookie, and analytics data

## Why we collect it

We collect information to:

- operate the platform
- authenticate users and keep accounts secure
- facilitate messaging, inspections, offers, and document sharing
- manage compliance, moderation, fraud prevention, and support
- process fees and bookkeeping
- improve product performance and reliability
- send operational notifications and, where permitted, marketing communications

## Direct marketing

Marketing communications should be consent-based where required. Users can change marketing preferences and unsubscribe from non-essential messaging.

## Who we may share data with

We may share information with:

- service providers hosting the platform or delivering communications
- payment processors
- mapping, storage, analytics, and security providers
- professional advisers or regulators where required
- lawyers, conveyancers, inspectors, and other professionals invited by a user
- other users to the extent needed to run listings, bookings, and offers

## Cross-border processing

Some service providers may process data outside Australia. Final published policy should name the actual vendors and overseas disclosure details used in production.

## Storage and security

We use layered security controls including role-based access, password hashing, signed URLs for sensitive documents, and auditing of sensitive actions. No system is perfectly secure, and users should also protect their credentials.

## Access, correction, and deletion

Users may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information, subject to legal, fraud, accounting, or dispute-retention needs.

## Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to run the service, resolve disputes, meet legal obligations, and protect the platform from fraud or abuse.

## Complaints

Privacy complaints should be submitted to the platform support contact. The final policy should include the nominated privacy contact and escalation options.

## Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes should be communicated through the app, website, or direct message to users where appropriate.
