# Complaints and Content Reporting Policy

## What can be reported

- misleading listings
- abusive or harassing messages
- scams or off-platform pressure
- privacy or copyright concerns
- unsafe inspection behavior

## Review process

Homeowner should acknowledge reports, triage risk, preserve evidence where appropriate, and communicate outcomes where possible.

## Possible actions

- request corrections
- remove content
- hide listing
- restrict messaging
- suspend account
- escalate to legal or law enforcement where appropriate
