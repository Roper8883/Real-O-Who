# Building and Pest Marketplace Terms

## Role of the platform

Homeowner acts as a booking and coordination intermediary only.

## Provider responsibility

Providers are responsible for their licensing, insurance, qualifications, service scope, and report content. Homeowner does not guarantee findings, availability, turnaround times, or suitability for a user's needs.

## Payments and refunds

If Homeowner collects service fees, refund rules should reflect provider cancellation policies, already-incurred costs, and consumer law rights.

## Reliance

Reports may contain scope limits, assumptions, or expiry periods. Users should read those limits carefully and obtain their own advice where needed.
