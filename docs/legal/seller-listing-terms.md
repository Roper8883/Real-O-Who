# Seller Listing Terms

## Authority to list

Sellers must only create listings if they own the property or are lawfully authorised to market it.

## Accuracy

Sellers must ensure, to the best of their knowledge, that listing facts, images, pricing statements, disclosures, and uploaded documents are accurate and not misleading.

## Jurisdiction documents

Sellers agree to upload or arrange the documents required or recommended for the relevant state or territory, including contracts, vendor statements, disclosure bundles, body corporate records, or inspection reports where applicable.

## Media standards

Media must fairly represent the property. Edited or staged content must not materially mislead buyers about condition, size, layout, inclusions, views, or defects.

## Offer integrity

Sellers must not fabricate offers, manipulate urgency, or falsely claim competing buyers.

## Inspection responsibilities

Sellers are responsible for safe inspection arrangements, lawful access, privacy of attendees, and clear safety instructions.

## Consequences

Homeowner may pause, correct, hide, or remove listings and may suspend seller accounts where authority, accuracy, legality, or safety concerns arise.
