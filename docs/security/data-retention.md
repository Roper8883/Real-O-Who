# Data Retention

## General policy

- operational data is retained while the account or listing remains active
- soft delete is preferred for records with legal, fraud, or support relevance
- deletion requests should be staged through review, approval, and completion

## Suggested baseline

- sessions and device history: 90 to 180 days
- audit logs: at least 2 years
- moderation and abuse reports: at least 2 years
- invoices and payment records: based on accounting obligations
- listing and offer histories: retained for support and dispute handling, subject to legal advice
