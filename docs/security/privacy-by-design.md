# Privacy By Design

## Product posture

The platform is designed to minimise unnecessary data exposure while still enabling trust between buyers and sellers.

## Core practices

- mask contact details in messaging until an explicit reveal workflow exists
- collect only the data required for listings, enquiries, inspections, and offers
- distinguish marketing consent from operational messaging
- store role-sensitive documents with separate access policies
- attach provenance and disclaimers wherever third-party enrichment is shown
