Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Real A Who

Effective date: March 31, 2026

Real A Who is designed to work without an account or online service. The app is a private reflection journal for prompts, notes, favorites, and simple mood and tag insights.

Information collected by the app

Real A Who does not collect personal information, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking data from within the app.

Journal entries and app content

Your journal entries, tags, favorites, and related app content are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded to Aaron Roper.

Third-party services

Real A Who does not include third-party advertising SDKs or third-party analytics SDKs.

Sharing

If you choose to share a reflection using the iOS share sheet, the content is shared only with the destination you select. Real A Who does not automatically transmit your entries anywhere.

Support communications

If you contact support by email or through the project issue tracker, the information you send will be used only to respond to your request and provide support.

Data retention

Because entries are stored locally on your device, you control how long they remain there. You can delete entries inside the app or remove the app from your device.

Children

Real A Who is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If it changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
