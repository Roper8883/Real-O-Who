# Product Requirements Document

## Mission

Build the best private residential property sale product for Australia. Homeowners should be able to list privately, while buyers still get rich search, trusted disclosure guidance, inspection coordination, and a clear path into legal review.

## Primary users

- Sellers / owners
- Buyers
- Building and pest inspection providers
- Admin, support, and compliance staff

## Core promise

- Private owners can market and manage residential property without a traditional selling agent.
- Buyers can search, compare, inspect, message the owner, request reports, and make structured non-binding offers.
- The platform assists with document collection and workflow coordination without acting as a law firm, conveyancer, escrow service, or trust account.

## V1 capability set

- accounts, roles, verification, and security settings
- seller dashboard and listing wizard
- listing search, detail pages, map-ready search UX, and saved properties
- direct owner messaging with moderation hooks
- owner-managed inspections and building/pest booking pathways
- non-binding offer workflow with counters and contract request stage
- document centre, disclosure bundle tracking, and admin/compliance tools
- first-party analytics and notification events

## Non-goals for V1

- auctions
- deposits or trust money handling
- contract finalisation inside the platform
- automatic legal advice or transaction safety conclusions
- escrow, settlement, or conveyancing replacement

## Success indicators

- sellers can publish state-aware private treaty listings
- buyers can confidently evaluate, inspect, and negotiate
- compliance staff can audit listing readiness and intervene early
- the product is credible enough to run as a real operational platform with staged provider integrations
