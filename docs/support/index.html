Support for Real A Who

If you need help with Real A Who, use the contact options below. The app is an offline reflection journal, so issue reports are most useful when they include device details and what you were doing when something went wrong.

What to include

  • Your device model.
  • Your iOS version.
  • A short description of the issue.
  • Steps to reproduce the issue, if relevant.

App overview

Real A Who stores journal entries locally on your device. No account is required, and no analytics or tracking SDKs are included in the app.
