# State-Based Buying and Selling Guide

Each Australian state and territory has different document, disclosure, and cooling-off settings. Homeowner uses state-aware rules to help structure the workflow, but legal advice should still come from a qualified professional.

- `NSW`: contract before advertising
- `VIC`: Section 32 before signing
- `QLD`: prescribed disclosure bundle and delivery proof
- `SA`: Form 1 and service timing
- `ACT`: draft contract and seller-provided reports
- `NT`: approved-form contract workflow support
- `WA`: no general statutory cooling-off by default
- `TAS`: buyer-beware and strong due diligence prompts
