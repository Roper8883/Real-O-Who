Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Real O Who

Effective date: April 15, 2026

Overview

Real O Who is a private property sale app that helps sellers and buyers manage listings, offers, secure messaging, legal handoff, post-settlement tasks, and related workflow tools.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Real O Who uses, how that information is used, and the choices available to you.

Information we collect

Real O Who processes information you provide directly, information created through your use of the app, and limited device-level settings needed to make features work. This can include:

  • Profile details, such as your name, email address, suburb, and whether you are using the app as a buyer or seller.
  • Local starter-profile data created when the app is used without a full sign-in flow.
  • Property listing details, photos or attachments you provide, inspection notes, saved searches, saved properties, and offer records.
  • Secure message content, message metadata, safety reports, blocks, and moderation-related actions inside transaction threads.
  • Verification information and supporting documents you choose to upload, such as ownership evidence, finance proof, contract packets, settlement summaries, handover files, invoices, quotes, and payment proof.
  • Legal workflow information, including selected conveyancers, solicitors, or lawyers, contract-status steps, and invitation activity.
  • Post-sale concierge workflow information, including provider selections, service schedules, follow-up reminders, issue logs, receipts, invoices, refunds, and archive records.
  • Notification preferences and reminder state used to send local task and follow-up alerts when you allow notifications.
  • Suburb, address area, coordinates, and provider search context needed to show local legal or concierge options.

How we use information

Real O Who uses information to operate the app and its transaction workflows, including to:

  • Show listings, saved items, offers, negotiations, legal steps, and archive history.
  • Enable direct communication between transaction participants.
  • Generate reminders, status updates, and in-app workflow prompts.
  • Store and present documents you upload or generate through the app.
  • Support local provider search, ranking, replacement suggestions, and booking follow-through.
  • Provide support, respond to abuse or safety reports, and improve service reliability.

How data is stored

Real O Who may operate in a local-device mode, a synced mode, or a combination of both depending on feature availability and backend support. App data can be stored locally on your device and, where supported, synchronized with backend services used to support the app experience.

Secure message content is stored in encrypted local app storage and may be included in protected transaction synchronization workflows where those workflows are available.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, Real O Who may send local device notifications for task reminders, concierge follow-ups, legal workflow steps, and other time-sensitive transaction events. You can manage notification permissions in your device settings.

Sharing inside a transaction

Information you choose to place into a transaction workflow may be visible to the other party in that workflow and to invited legal participants connected to that same transaction. Examples include offer details, selected providers, messages, uploaded documents, and settlement-related records.

Real O Who does not sell your personal information and does not provide your personal information to advertisers for their own marketing use.

Third-party services and external links

Real O Who may open external services or links when you use provider-search, website, phone, map, or support features. Those services may process information under their own terms and privacy policies.

For example, when you look up local conveyancers, solicitors, lawyers, removalists, cleaners, or related providers, the relevant suburb or area details may be used to retrieve local results or open an external listing.

Support communications

If you contact support by email, we use the information you send to respond to your request and maintain support records.

Data retention and deletion

Data may remain on your device while the app is installed. Removing the app may remove locally stored data from that device, subject to your device backup behavior.

If synced services or account deletion tools are available in the app, those tools may also remove or update records associated with your app use. Some records may be retained for legitimate operational, safety, legal, or dispute-resolution reasons.

Security

Real O Who uses reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect app data. No storage or transmission method is guaranteed to be completely secure, so you should avoid uploading information you are not comfortable managing digitally.

Children

Real O Who is not directed to children and is not intended for use by people under 18 in connection with property transactions.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The current version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
