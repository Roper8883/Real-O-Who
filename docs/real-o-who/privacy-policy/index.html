Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Real O Who

Effective date: April 7, 2026

Overview

Real O Who is an offline reflection journal for writing private notes, saving favorites, and viewing simple on-device insights.

Information collected by the app

Real O Who does not collect personal information, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking data from within the app.

Your entries and related content

Your reflections, moods, favorites, and tags are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded to Aaron Roper.

Third-party services

Real O Who does not include third-party advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or account-based backend services in this version of the app.

Sharing

If you choose to share a reflection using the iOS share sheet, the content is shared only with the destination you select. Real O Who does not automatically transmit your entries anywhere.

Support communications

If you contact support by email, the information you send will be used only to respond to your request and provide support.

Data retention

Because entries are stored locally on your device, you control how long they remain there. You can delete entries in the app or remove the app from your device.

Children

Real O Who is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If it changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
