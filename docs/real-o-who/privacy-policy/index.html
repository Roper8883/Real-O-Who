Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Real O Who

Effective date: April 9, 2026

Overview

Real O Who helps homeowners and buyers run private property sales end-to-end, including listing management, secure direct messaging, offer negotiation, and legal handoff workflow.

Information collected by the app

We collect and process data you provide and generate in the app, including:

  • Account details you enter, such as name, email address, role (buyer/seller), and suburb.
  • Listings, offers, saved searches, inspection plans, and contract-related notes you create.
  • Messages and reporting actions inside secure sale threads.
  • Selected legal representative details and legal workspace invite activity.
  • Location-related suburb and property coordinates used for local legal-professional search.

How your data is stored and used

Real O Who runs in two modes depending on backend availability. When available, your profile, listings, offers, conversations, and sale workflow data can sync across devices via our backend service. Data is also kept in local app storage as a fallback for single-device or offline use.

Message content is stored in an encrypted local vault in the app and transmitted only as part of sale thread synchronization where supported.

Third-party services

Real O Who uses links to external map/place services when you search for nearby conveyancers and solicitors. Those services may process the suburb/property area details you provide only for that search.

Sharing

Listing summaries, messages, and contract packets are shared only with users participating in the same sale workflow, and optionally through your selected legal representative within that workflow.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not provide your data to advertisers.

Support communications

If you contact support by email, the information you send will be used only to respond to your request and provide support.

Data retention

You can remove the app to clear local data at any time. If you use backend sync, account deletion from within the app also removes your account records and associated sale/workspace state from the local backend dataset.

Children

Real O Who is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If it changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
