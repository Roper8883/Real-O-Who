Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Real O Who

Effective date: April 8, 2026

Overview

Real O Who is a private property marketplace prototype for browsing listings, managing seller workflows, and exchanging buyer-seller messages.

Information collected by the app

In the current prototype build, Real O Who does not transmit account data, analytics events, advertising identifiers, or tracking data to Aaron Roper from within the app.

Your listings, offers, and messages

Listings, saved homes, inspection plans, offer records, and current conversation data are stored locally on your device in this version of the app. Conversation data is written to an encrypted local vault.

Third-party services

Real O Who does not include third-party advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or a hosted multi-device messaging backend in this prototype version.

Sharing

If future versions allow users to share listing details or export conversations, the content will be shared only to the destination chosen by the user. The current prototype does not automatically transmit your listing or conversation data to Aaron Roper.

Support communications

If you contact support by email, the information you send will be used only to respond to your request and provide support.

Data retention

Because the current build stores data locally on your device, you control how long the information remains there. You can remove the app and its data from your device at any time.

Children

Real O Who is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If it changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
