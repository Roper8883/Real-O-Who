Real O Who

A private property marketplace for direct buyer and seller conversations.

Real O Who is being rebuilt as a private-sale real estate app inspired by the buyer journeys on Australia’s major property platforms. The current prototype includes property search, saved homes, inspection planning, direct offers, and encrypted local buyer-seller messaging.

Privacy Policy Terms of Use Support

What the app does

Search and shortlist

Browse private listings, filter by suburb and home type, and save promising properties.

Seller tools

Create listings, track direct offers, and manage inspection-ready private sale workflows.

Encrypted messaging

Buyer and seller conversations are stored in an encrypted local vault in the current prototype build.
