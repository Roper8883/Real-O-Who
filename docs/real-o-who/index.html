Real O Who

A simple offline reflection journal for iPhone and iPad.

Real O Who gives you a daily prompt, a quiet place to write, and a private archive of your reflections. The app works immediately on launch with no account, no ads, and no analytics.

Privacy Policy Terms of Use Support

What the app does

Daily prompts

Start with a prompt, then write freely in your own words.

Local storage

Your entries, moods, tags, and favorites stay on your device.

On-device insights

Simple summaries are generated locally from the reflections you save.
